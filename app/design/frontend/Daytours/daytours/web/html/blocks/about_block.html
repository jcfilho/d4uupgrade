About Buenos Aires

Argentina is a travel treasure; one of the world’s most alluring destinations. It is synonymous with sophisticated cultural attractions, outstanding natural beauty, and wonderfully diverse wildlife.

We can show you exactly why this country is so special. Journey north to soak in the majestic grandeur of Iguazu Falls and the spellbinding white wonder of Salinas Grandes; visit beautiful Mendoza to sample exquisite local wines and explore the spectacular Andean Cordillera; and in the cosmopolitan capital, Buenos Aires, bountiful museums and seductive tango rhythms await.

Further down south, across the hinterland of Patagonia, it’s adventure all the way – go mini-trekking on the ancient ice of Perito Moreno, embark on a Beagle Channel maritime excursion in Ushuaia, and discover breathtaking Victoria Island and the magical Arrayanes National Park in Bariloche. Our catalogue of tours in Argentina has something for every traveller.
