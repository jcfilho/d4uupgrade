{{trans "Can I book the tour when I get to Buenos Aires?"}}

{{trans "The products and tourist services of Bsas4u can be booked online wherever you are, in Buenos Aires or elsewhere. Our calendar will allow you to book up to two days in advance. Please contact our Support Team to verify availability and book over the telephone."}}

{{trans "I don't know what date I'm travelling yet, can I book a product and leave the date open?"}}

{{trans "We need a specific date to reserve any tour or service offered on our website. If you haven't decided on the specific dates you are going to stay in Buenos Aires, you can book a provisional date for the tour service you want (excluding tickets for football games or any other special events) and subsequently change it up to 3 days before the desired date via email with our Support Team."}}

{{trans "I already paid and I'm not sure if my booking was received. What should I do?"}}

{{trans "You should receive an automatic response from our website confirming that your booking is in process. However, our Operations Team has to confirm this reservation with the tour provider, and, therefore, you should receive a second e-mail within 24 hours of your booking (up to 48 hours on weekends and national holidays) confirming the details of the service including pickup time, customer emergency number, and the voucher of your service. If you haven't received such an email, please check your junk folder or contact us via Chat to verify the status of your order."}}

{{trans "How can I contact you if I have a last minute event/emergency before my tour?"}}

{{trans "You will receive with your voucher, a 24/7 mobile number to call in Buenos Aires in case you need to contact us out of office hours.
